Quotations and Citations

Browsers usually insert quotation marks around the q DOM.

H4H's goal is to build a world in which everyone has a decent place to live.

Browsers usually indent blockquote elements.

For nearly 60 years, WWF has been protecting the future of nature. The world's leading conservation organization, WWF works in over 100 countries and is supported by more than 1,000,000 members in the United States and close to 5,000,000 globally.

The address element defines contact information of a document.

Alice & Bob
123 Main St
Anywhere US 12345-6789

Mark abbreviations with the abbr tag.

The USA was founded in 1776.

The cite element usually is italicized.

If your browser supports bi-directional override, the following will be written right-to-left (rtl):

United States of America
